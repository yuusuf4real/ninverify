## 📊 Market Research: NIN Verification Use Cases in Nigeria

### Mandatory NIN Requirements (2024-2026)

#### 1. **Banking & Financial Services** 🏦
- **Bank Account Opening:** Mandatory since Dec 2023 (CBN directive)
- **BVN Linking:** Required for all Tier 1 accounts
- **Mobile Money/Wallets:** Mandatory for all operators
- **Tax ID (TIN):** NIN serves as TIN for individuals
- **Market Size:** 146M+ active bank customers
- **Deadline:** January 1, 2026 (strict enforcement)

#### 2. **Education & Examinations** 📚
- **JAMB Registration:** Mandatory for UTME/DE candidates
- **WAEC Registration:** Required for SSCE candidates
- **NECO Registration:** Mandatory for all candidates
- **NYSC Registration:** Required for all corps members (2024+)
- **Scholarship Applications:** NIN verification required
- **School Admissions:** Many institutions now require NIN
- **Market Size:** 5M+ students annually

#### 3. **Government Services** 🏛️
- **Nigerian Passport:** Mandatory since Jan 1, 2018
- **Driver's License:** Required for new applications/renewals
- **Voter Registration:** Recommended (not yet mandatory)
- **National ID Card:** NIN is the basis
- **Tax Clearance:** NIN required
- **Government Employment:** Mandatory for all civil servants
- **Market Size:** 10M+ applications annually

#### 4. **Telecommunications** 📱
- **SIM Card Registration:** Mandatory NIN-SIM linkage
- **SIM Card Activation:** Cannot activate without NIN
- **Market Size:** 200M+ mobile subscribers

#### 5. **Travel & Immigration** ✈️
- **International Passport:** Mandatory requirement
- **Visa Applications:** Often requires NIN verification
- **Immigration Services:** NIN verification needed
- **Market Size:** 2M+ passport applications annually

#### 6. **Employment & Professional Services** 💼
- **Job Applications:** Many employers require NIN
- **Professional Certifications:** Required by regulatory bodies
- **Pension Registration:** NIN mandatory
- **Health Insurance (NHIS):** NIN required
- **Market Size:** 5M+ formal sector workers

#### 7. **Real Estate & Legal** 🏠
- **Property Registration:** Increasingly required
- **Legal Documentation:** NIN verification for contracts
- **Notary Services:** NIN verification needed
- **Market Size:** 1M+ transactions annually
